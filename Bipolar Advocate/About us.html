I don't just guide you from the sidelines—I stand with you as your advocate to protect your rights and your future.

— My Credentials —

The Expertise Behind the Advocacy

While my passion is rooted in lived experience, my advocacy is built on a foundation of professional training and strategic expertise. I have dedicated myself to understanding the intricate details of mental health laws and healthcare systems to provide the high-level support my clients deserve.

Professional Advocacy Training:

My foundation is built on specialized education and rigorous training focused on navigating the complex intersection of medical and legal hurdles. I have mastered the strategies necessary to advocate for patient rights, ensuring that care received is not only appropriate but also legally sound during times of vulnerability.

System Navigation Expertise:

System Navigation Expertise: I possess a deep, specialized knowledge of how the healthcare and legal systems intersect, particularly for those living with Bipolar Disorder. This expertise allows me to bridge the gap between clinical providers and legal protections, helping clients move through the system with clarity and confidence rather than confusion.

Strategic Crisis Management:

Crises require a calm and experienced hand. I am specifically trained to act as a direct advocate for families during high-stress situations such as hospitalizations or unexpected legal challenges. My role is to provide real-time advocacy, stepping in to manage communications and ensure your loved one’s voice is heard.

Continuous Education:

The landscape of mental health law and healthcare resources is constantly evolving. I am committed to continuous education, staying regularly updated on the latest resources and rights-based protections to ensure that the guidance I provide is always based on the most current legal standards and clinical best practices.
